# Learning Path (by progress, not by calendar)

Advance sequential by Module. Your rhythm is defined by you; The criterion to advance is complete Results, not weeks.

## Recommended Module Order

### Phase 1 — Fundamentos

1. Programming and math for ML
1. Data collection, cleaning & visualization
1. Machine learning fundamentals

### Phase 2 — Modelado moderno

1. Deep learning basics
1. NLP & large language models
1. Computer vision

### Phase 3 — Especializaciones

1. Time series & anomaly detection
1. Recommender systems
1. Generative AI & prompt engineering

### Phase 4 — Production and responsibility

1. Ethics, bias & explainability
1. Data privacy & security
1. MLOps & AI in production

## Step by step inside each Module

1. Read the Module's `README.md` to understand Objective and scope.
1. Study `theory/README.md` and record synthesis in `notes/`.
1. Execute `examples/` to understand flow base.
1. Resolve Practices from `practices/` in order.
1. Complete `mini-project/` with reproducible Results.
1. Close `evaluation/` and check minimum 70/100.
1. Actualizar `STATUS.md` (level, bloqueos, next steps).

## Rule to move to the next Module

No avanzar hasta cumplir:

- Theory resumida.
- Practices completas.
- Mini-project validated.
- Evaluation aprobada.
- Lessons learned documented.
